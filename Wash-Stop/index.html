Who We Are

Washtop is a comunity based Laundromat Cafe located in the beatiful city of Bristol on Gloucester road, one of the most expressive and artistic roads Bristol has to offer.

Come and join us

While your clothes are getting freshly washed, why not stop for a coffee, chat and some of our home made goods served to you by our lovely staff! Washstop is not your normal laundromat cafe, we are a community who beleive in giving back to the community. We also have a stage area for live music and performances!

Community Events

Every monday we host an open mic night, that runs from 8pm until midnight! All abilities are welcome and all forms of spoken artistic expression from music to poetry are welcomed with open arms. Just turn up and play, click bellow for the poster.
